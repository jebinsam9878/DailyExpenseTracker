Why this app?

Most people don’t overspend because they are careless – they overspend because they can’t see the full picture every day. This tracker makes that picture impossible to ignore.

In a few clicks you’ll know: “How much did I really spend on food this month?”, “Where is my money silently leaking?” and “Am I actually safe to spend this weekend?”.

Real-time overview

Your dashboard lights up today’s spend, fresh transactions, and quick filters so you can spot trends in seconds, not weeks.

Smart reports

Beautiful monthly charts and category breakdowns answer the big questions: what’s growing, what to cut, and where you’re winning.

Personal space

Each user has a secure, personal workspace, with an admin view only for you to monitor usage, protect data, and build better habits over time.
